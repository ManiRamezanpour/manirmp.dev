Raycast is an awesome productivity tool

Raycast is a fast and customizable launcher that allows users to complete tasks, perform calculations, share links, and more.
It is designed to enhance productivity on macOS by providing a convenient way to access and control various functions and tools.
With Raycast, users can hold down the command key to view numbered options alongside search results, making it easier to navigate and perform actions efficiently.
